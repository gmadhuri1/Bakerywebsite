About Us

Madhu's Bakery is a quintessential bakery established in 2021 to bring heavenly and luxurious desserts to the City of Destiny. Our chefs are devoted to preparing gourmet delicacies and fresh pastries catered to the customer’s preferences. A rich blend of French and Indian cuisine, our pastries and baked goods strive to offer perfection.
We have opened our first branch in 2021 and we provide a wide range of services to hospitality institutions, caterers and as well as home deliveries.

Our Philosophy

We aim to:

  1. Provide a professional and customer-oriented service
  2. Maintain a presence throughout the state
  3. Promote an appetite for fresh cakes and healthy bread
  4. Make customers feel welcome at all outlets
  5. Constantly embrace innovation to meet our customers’ expectations
  6. Create an ecosystem to train and supply staff to the baking & confectionery industry
